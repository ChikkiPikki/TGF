What is Big Heart Backpacks?
Big Heart Backpacks is a non-profit organization that buys backpacks for children in need and fills them with necessities such as: water, snacks, first aid kits, feminine hygiene products, school supplies, down jackets, lightweight blankets, solar powered light bulbs, and other essentials.

Our team organizes trips to rural areas and villages in need to personally deliver the backpacks.

All of this is made possibly by you and your generous donations.
